ABOUT DRIED FOODS BILTONG
Founded in the heart of Chitungwiza, DRIED FOODS BILTONG is a fast-growing company dedicated to delivering the finest biltong to Zimbabwe and beyond.

Our journey began with a simple passion for quality, flavor, and tradition. Today, we blend time-honored recipes with modern techniques to create biltong that's both authentic and unforgettable.

We believe in supporting local farmers, using only the best cuts of beef, and slow-curing our biltong to perfection. Join us as we grow, one delicious bite at a time!
